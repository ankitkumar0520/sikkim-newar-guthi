Naya Samman

Nawaan (Newar) culture and civilization brought new respect and honor.

Preserving the Timeless Newar Identity and Celebrating Community Spirit Through Festivals, Rituals, and Togetherness in Sikkim

The Sikkim Newar Guthi is a vibrant community institution that preserves and promotes the age-old cultural, social, and religious practices of the Newar people. From organizing grand festivals like Indra Jatra to supporting families in rituals and community events, the Guthi plays a central role in keeping the rich Newar heritage alive in the land of Sikkim.
